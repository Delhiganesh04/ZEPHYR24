About Zephyr'24

Zephyr'24 is an annual symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering at Sri Sairam Engineering College. It is an event that brings together the brightest minds in the industry to showcase their skills, share knowledge, and compete in various technical events. Join us for an experience of innovation, collaboration, and learning.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in Electronics and Instrumentation, nurturing technically competent professionals.

Our Mission

We strive to impart quality education and foster research and development in the field of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering.

Achievements

Our students have won numerous accolades in national and international competitions in the field of engineering and technology.
